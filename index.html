Essential Safety Tips

Be prepared before emergencies strike

📱

Save Emergency Contacts

Store all emergency numbers (112, 108, 100, 101, 1091) in your phone with the ICE (In Case of Emergency) prefix.

🏠

Emergency Kit at Home

Keep a basic first aid kit, torch, extra medicines, water, and important documents ready at all times.

🗺️

Know Your Exits

Identify emergency exits in your building, workplace, and public spaces. Practice evacuation drills.

👨‍👩‍👧‍👦

Family Safety Plan

Create a family emergency plan with a designated meeting point and contact person outside the affected area.
